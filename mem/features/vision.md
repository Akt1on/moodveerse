---
name: Vision & tone
description: Core product purpose, emotional contract with the user, what MoodVerse must always feel like
type: feature
---
**Vision:** MoodVerse is an empathetic literary companion that helps users deeply process their emotions through highly resonant real poetry, literary excerpts, and film quotes. Every match should feel profoundly personal and healing.

**Emotional contract:**
- Warm, poetic, supportive — never clinical, never therapeutic-jargon, never generic affirmations.
- The user describes how they feel; the app responds with literature that *understands* them.
- Tone in Russian by default; literary register, tender, unhurried.

**Never do:**
- Generic motivational quotes.
- Pop-psychology language ("you've got this", "stay positive").
- Cold/analytical responses.
- Surface-level keyword matching ("sad" → sad poem).
